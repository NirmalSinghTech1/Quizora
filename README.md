# Quizora

## Description
Quizora is a free quiz app that allows users to test their knowledge of programming languages through 10 engaging quiz questions. After completing the quiz, users can save their scores to track progress over time.
The app is powered by the `Quiz API`, which provides a wide range of free programming-related questions and data.

 Quizora not only challenges your skills but also helps you learn faster by offering detailed explanations for each question. When you select an option, the app explains the reasoning behind the correct answer, giving you valuable insights and related information.
This way, even if you choose the wrong option, youâ€™ll understand the concept clearly and strengthen your learning experience. Quizora makes technical learning fun, interactive, and effective


## How to use
- Click the **Play** Button to start the quiz.
- Answer each quiz question as it appears.
- You must answer **10 questions**, with **30 seconds** allowed for each.
- Every correct answer increases your score by **10 points**.
- After completing the quiz, you can save your progress by entering your name.
